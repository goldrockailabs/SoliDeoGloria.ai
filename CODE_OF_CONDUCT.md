# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful,
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of denomination,
theological perspective, age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity,
level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior
- Respectful engagement across theological differences
- Charitable interpretation of others' positions
- Constructive feedback focused on improving the benchmark
- Welcoming newcomers from all Christian traditions
- Acknowledging the legitimacy of diverse perspectives

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Disparaging other Christian traditions
- Claiming one tradition as exclusively valid
- Harassment or personal attacks
- Trolling or inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information

## Theological Engagement

This benchmark includes perspectives from Catholic, Orthodox, and
Protestant traditions. Contributors should:

1. Represent other traditions accurately and fairly
2. Avoid caricaturing positions they disagree with
3. Acknowledge areas of genuine theological disagreement
4. Focus on what unites Christians where possible
5. Respect the sincerity of different denominational commitments

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will remove, edit, or reject contributions that
violate this Code of Conduct. Repeated violations may result in
temporary or permanent bans.

## Reporting

Report violations to the project maintainers via GitHub issues or
private message.
